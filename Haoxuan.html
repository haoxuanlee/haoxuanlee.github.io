Biography

I am currently a graduate student at the College of Chemistry and Molecular Engineering, Peking University, specializing in Catalytic Chemistry. My research journey is driven by a passion for understanding the fundamental mechanisms of surface reactions and designing efficient catalysts for sustainable energy applications.

My current work focuses on the design of novel heterogeneous catalysts, specifically exploring the structure-activity relationships at the atomic level. I utilize a combination of advanced spectroscopy techniques and kinetic studies to unravel reaction pathways.

Outside of the lab, I enjoy coding, reading scientific history, and playing badminton.
